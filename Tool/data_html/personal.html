Welcome to the webpage of Dr. Junming Duan. He is currently a Humboldt Research Fellow at the Institute of Mathematics, University of Würzburg, working with Prof. Dr. Christian Klingenberg. Until September 2023, he was a postdoctoral researcher at MCSS, EPFL, working with Prof. Jan S. Hesthaven. He received his B.Sc. (2016) and Ph.D. (2021) at Peking University, in the School of Mathematical Sciences, supervised by Prof. Huazhong Tang. His research primarily involves high-order accurate numerical methods and reduced-order modeling.

Research Interests

  • Numerical methods for hyperbolic conservation laws
  • Computational fluid dynamics
  • High-order accurate numerical methods
  • Structure-preserving methods
  • Moving mesh methods
  • Active flux methods
  • Model order reduction
  • Reduced-order modeling
  • Machine-learning-enhanced data-driven methods
